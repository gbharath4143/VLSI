1. Go to the website

https://www.edaplayground.com

2. Choose a Simulator

At the top left, under "Choose a Simulator"

Pick Model Sim (or any other like VCS, Icarus, etc.)

3. Write Your Code

Left side = Design file (e.g., Verilog/VHDL)

Right side = Testbench (to simulate your design)

4. Click "Run"

Hit the blue "Run" button on top.

Output will show below.

5. Optional: Save & Share

Create a free account to save your code or share links with others.
